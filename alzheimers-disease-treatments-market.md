<p><p>The Alzheimer's Disease treatments market primarily includes Cholinesterase Inhibitors and NMDA Antagonists. Cholinesterase Inhibitors, such as Donepezil and Rivastigmine, enhance communication between nerve cells by increasing acetylcholine levels, thus improving cognitive function. NMDA Antagonists, like Memantine, regulate glutamate activity to protect brain cells against excessive stimulation, aiding in the management of moderate to severe Alzheimer's. Together, these treatment classes address various symptoms and stages of the disease, providing symptomatic relief and potential disease progression management.</p></p>
