<p><p>The automotive rear-view camera systems market comprises two primary types: wired and wireless systems. Wired rear-view camera systems typically involve a direct cable connection between the camera and the display, providing reliable signal transmission with minimal interference. In contrast, wireless rear-view camera systems utilize radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology to transmit images, offering easier installation and flexibility but may be more susceptible to interference. Both types enhance driver safety by providing a clearer view of the vehicle's surroundings while reversing.</p></p>
