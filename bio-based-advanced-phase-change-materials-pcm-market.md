The market is categorized into two types: metallic and non-metallic materials. Metallic PCMs, often utilizing materials like alloys, offer high thermal conductivity and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid heat transfer. Non-metallic materials, which include organic compounds and composites, provide versatility, lower density, and are typically more environmentally friendly. Both types enhance energy efficiency in various applications, from building materials to thermal storage systems.</p></p>
