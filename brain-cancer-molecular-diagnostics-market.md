<p><p>The brain cancer molecular diagnostics market encompasses various technologies for accurate disease detection and characterization. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling identification of genetic mutations. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) visualizes specific DNA regions using fluorescent probes, aiding in chromosomal abnormalities detection. DNA sequencing provides detailed information on genetic variations, facilitating personalized treatment approaches. Gene chips, or microarrays, allow simultaneous analysis of thousands of genes, identifying expression patterns relevant to tumor biology. Together, these technologies enhance diagnostic precision and therapeutic strategies.</p></p>
