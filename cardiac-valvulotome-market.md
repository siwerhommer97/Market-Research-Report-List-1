<p><ul><li>Expandable Cardiac Valvulotome</li><li>Over the Wire Cardiac Valvulotome</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac valvulotome market comprises primarily two types: expandable cardiac valvulotome and over the wire cardiac valvulotome. Expandable cardiac valvulotomes are designed to be deployed via a catheter and expand to create precise incisions in heart valves, enhancing access and reducing trauma. In contrast, over the wire cardiac valvulotomes utilize a guide wire for navigation and placement, allowing for more controlled positioning and flexibility during procedures. Both types aim to improve cardiac interventions by facilitating minimally invasive approaches.</p></p>
