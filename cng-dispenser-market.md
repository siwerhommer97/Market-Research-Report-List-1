<p><p>The CNG dispenser market consists of two primary types: Fast Fill and Time Fill. Fast Fill dispensers provide a rapid refueling option, allowing vehicles to refuel quickly, making them ideal for commercial fleets with high turnaround needs. In contrast, Time Fill dispensers are designed for overnight or extended refueling, where vehicles are refueled over several hours, typically used in fleet operations with predictable schedules. Both types cater to different operational requirements in the compressed natural gas sector.</p></p>
