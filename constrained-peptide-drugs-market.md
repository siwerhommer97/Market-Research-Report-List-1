<p><p>The Constrained Peptide Drugs market comprises various types, primarily cyclic peptides and disulfide-rich peptides (DRPs). Cyclic peptides feature a circular structure that enhances stability and bioavailability, making them effective therapeutics. Disulfide-rich peptides, characterized by internal disulfide bonds, exhibit increased resistance to enzymatic degradation and improved binding affinity. Both types leverage unique structural properties to target specific receptors, leading to advancements in drug design and therapeutic applications across various diseases, including cancer and metabolic disorders.</p></p>
