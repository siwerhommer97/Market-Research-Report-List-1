<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crankshaft position sensor market includes several types: linear position sensors, rotary position sensors, and proximity sensors. Linear position sensors measure the straight-line position of the crankshaft, providing precise feedback. Rotary position sensors track the angular position, crucial for timing and engine performance. Proximity sensors detect the crankshaft's presence or absence without direct contact, enhancing durability and reliability. Each type plays a vital role in engine management systems, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency in modern vehicles.</p></p>
