<p><strong>The Dielectric Multiplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEM Mode</li><li>TE Mode</li><li>TM Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dielectric multiplexer market is categorized into three main types based on electromagnetic wave propagation modes: TEM (Transverse Electromagnetic) mode, TE (Transverse Electric) mode, and TM (Transverse Magnetic) mode. TEM mode utilizes both electric and magnetic fields perpendicular to the direction of propagation, offering broad bandwidth applications. TE mode features electromagnetic waves with no electric field component in the direction of propagation, while TM mode includes no magnetic field component. Each mode serves distinct applications in communication systems and signal processing.</p></p>
