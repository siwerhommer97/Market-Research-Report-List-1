<p><strong>The Double Deck Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traction</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double deck elevator market features two primary types: traction and hydraulic systems. Traction elevators utilize cables and pulleys, allowing for energy-efficient vertical movement, ideal for high-rise buildings. They provide smooth travel with faster speeds, accommodating varying building heights. Hydraulic elevators, on the other hand, rely on fluid-driven pistons, suitable for mid-rise applications. They offer simple installation and cost-effectiveness but are generally slower and require more space. Both systems cater to specific building needs and user preferences, enhancing vertical transportation efficiency.</p></p>
