<p><ul><li>Ductless Filtered Storage Cabinets</li><li>Ducted Filtered Storage Cabinets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtered storage cabinets are designed to safely store hazardous materials while minimizing exposure to contaminants. The market is generally divided into two main types: ductless filtered storage cabinets and ducted filtered storage cabinets. Ductless cabinets utilize built-in filters to purify air before recirculating it within the cabinet, making them suitable for smaller spaces. In contrast, ducted cabinets connect to an external ventilation system, effectively expelling harmful fumes outside, ideal for larger facilities requiring stringent ventilation standards.</p></p>
