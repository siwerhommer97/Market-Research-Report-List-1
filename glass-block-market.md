<p><strong>The Glass Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered Glass Block</li><li>Annealed Glass Block</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass block market is primarily divided into two types: tempered glass blocks and annealed glass blocks. Tempered glass blocks are heat-treated to enhance strength and durability, making them ideal for high-impact applications and safety purposes. Conversely, annealed glass blocks are produced through a standard cooling process, resulting in a softer, more flexible product, often used for decorative purposes due to their aesthetic appeal. Both types serve distinct roles in construction and design, addressing different functional and aesthetic needs.</p></p>
