<p><p>The high-speed hand dryer market comprises two main types: single side jet hand dryers and double-sided jet hand dryers. Single side jet hand dryers feature a focused airflow that dries hands from one direction, making them suitable for compact spaces. In contrast, double-sided jet hand dryers utilize dual airflow, effectively drying hands from both sides simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and speed. Both types are energy-efficient alternatives to paper towels, promoting hygiene and reducing waste in public and commercial restrooms.</p></p>
