<p><p>The High-Throughput Single Cell Sorting Platform market includes two primary types: Flow Cytometry Sorting Platforms and Microfluidic Chip Cell Sorting Platforms. Flow Cytometry Sorting uses laser technology to analyze and sort cells based on specific characteristics like size and fluorescence, enabling rapid processing of large cell populations. In contrast, Microfluidic Chip Cell Sorting employs miniaturized channels to manipulate fluid flow for precise cell handling, allowing for better control and efficiency, making both effective for applications in genomics and personalized medicine.</p></p>
