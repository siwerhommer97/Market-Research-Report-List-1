<p><p>The high-voltage power cable market is categorized into three main types: underground, overhead, and submarine. Underground cables are buried to reduce visual impact and enhance safety, making them ideal for urban areas. Overhead cables are suspended on towers or poles, offering a cost-effective solution for long distances but occupying more land space. Submarine cables, laid on the seabed, are essential for inter island connections and international power trading, facilitating energy transmission across water bodies efficiently.</p></p>
