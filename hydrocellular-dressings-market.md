<p><p>The hydrocellular dressings market is divided into two main types: non-adhesive and adhesive hydrocellular dressings. Non-adhesive hydrocellular dressings are designed to be secured onto wounds using secondary dressings, providing flexibility in application while minimizing trauma during dressing changes. Conversely, adhesive hydrocellular dressings feature an integrated adhesive layer that enables direct application to the skin, offering convenience and stability without the need for additional securing methods. Both types are utilized for their moisture-retentive properties, promoting optimal wound healing.</p></p>
