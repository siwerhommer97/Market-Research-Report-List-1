<p><p>The intracranial aneurysm treatment market comprises various approaches: Surgical Clipping involves sealing the aneurysm with a metal clip through open surgery, providing a long-term solution. Endovascular Coiling employs a minimally invasive method, inserting coils to induce clotting within the aneurysm. Flow Diverters are specialized stents that redirect blood flow and promote healing of the aneurysm. Other treatments may include newer techniques and technologies, catering to individual patient needs and varying aneurysm characteristics. Each method has distinct advantages and application scenarios.</p></p>
