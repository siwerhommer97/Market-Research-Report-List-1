<p><p>Intraoperative Neurophysiologic Monitoring (IONM) services focus on safeguarding neurological function during surgery. The market includes three main types: Electroencephalography (EEG), which monitors brain activity to detect any abnormal changes; Electromyography (EMG), assessing muscle response and nerve function; and Evoked Potentials (EP), measuring the brain's electrical activity in response to stimuli to evaluate sensory pathways. These modalities collectively enhance surgical safety by providing real-time feedback on the patient’s neurological status during procedures.</p></p>
