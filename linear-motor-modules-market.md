<p><ul><li>Iron Core Linear Motors</li><li>Iron Less Linear Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear motor modules market comprises two primary types: iron core linear motors and ironless linear motors. Iron core linear motors utilize a magnetic core to enhance performance and generate high thrust and power density, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, ironless linear motors feature lightweight designs and reduced cogging, offering smoother motion and higher precision, ideal for applications requiring accuracy and speed. Both types cater to various industries, including automation, robotics, and transportation.</p></p>
