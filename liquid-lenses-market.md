<p><ul><li>Electrowetting-Based Liquid Lense</li><li>Liquid Crystal Lense</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid lenses market primarily features electrowetting-based liquid lenses and liquid crystal lenses. Electrowetting-based lenses utilize electric fields to manipulate liquid droplets, allowing for rapid focus changes and adjustable optical properties. They are ideal for applications requiring dynamic adjustments. Liquid crystal lenses, on the other hand, use liquid crystal materials that change refractive index under an electric field, enabling finer control over light transmission. Both types are gaining traction in sectors such as consumer electronics, automotive, and medical imaging due to their versatility and compact design.</p></p>
