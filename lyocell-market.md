<p><p>The Lyocell market is primarily divided into two types: Normal Lyocell Fiber and Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber. Normal Lyocell Fiber is known for its softness, breathability, and eco-friendliness, making it popular in apparel and home textiles. Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber, on the other hand, has enhanced durability and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for technical applications and performance textiles. Both types leverage the sustainability of wood-based resources, but they serve different functional needs in various industries.</p></p>
