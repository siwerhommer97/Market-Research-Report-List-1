<p><ul><li>Thawing Chambers</li><li>Roller Thawing Machine</li><li>Thawing Continuous Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave thawing equipment market includes three main types: thawing chambers, roller thawing machines, and thawing continuous lines. Thawing chambers are enclosed spaces that use microwave technology to uniformly thaw food items. Roller thawing machines feature rotating surfaces that provide efficient heat distribution, ideal for smaller batches. Thawing continuous lines are extensive systems designed for high-capacity operations, allowing for seamless, continuous thawing of large quantities of food, ensuring quick processing and minimal handling for industrial applications.</p></p>
