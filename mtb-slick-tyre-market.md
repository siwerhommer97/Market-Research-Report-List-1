<p><p>The MTB slick tyre market is categorized into two main types: MTB slicks and MTB semi-slicks. MTB slicks feature a completely smooth tread design, providing minimal rolling resistance and optimal performance on paved surfaces, making them ideal for urban riding and racing. In contrast, MTB semi-slicks have a tread pattern with moderate knobs, offering better grip on mixed terrain while still allowing for efficient rolling on smoother paths. This versatility appeals to riders seeking performance across various surfaces.</p></p>
