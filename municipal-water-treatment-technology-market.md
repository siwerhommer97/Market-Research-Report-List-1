<p><strong>The Municipal Water Treatment Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis Membranes</li><li>Ion-Exchange</li><li>Ultrafiltration (UF) Membranes</li><li>Electrodeionization (EDI)</li><li>Biological Filtration</li><li>Chemical Conditioning of Water</li><li>Clarification, Filtration and Chlorination</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The municipal water treatment technology market comprises various methods for ensuring safe drinking water. Reverse osmosis membranes effectively remove contaminants by forcing water through semi-permeable membranes. Ion-exchange removes dissolved ions, while ultrafiltration (UF) membranes filter out particles and bacteria. Electrodeionization (EDI) combines ion-exchange and electric processes for purification. Biological filtration utilizes microorganisms to break down impurities. Chemical conditioning adjusts water chemistry, and clarification separates solids from liquids. Filtration and chlorination are vital for disinfection, ensuring water safety. Other technologies may include advanced filtration systems and treatment innovations.</p></p>
