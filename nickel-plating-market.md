<p><strong>The Nickel Plating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroless Nickel Plating</li><li>Electro Nickel Plating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel plating market comprises two primary types: electroless nickel plating and electro nickel plating. Electroless nickel plating involves a chemical reaction that deposits nickel uniformly on surfaces without electrical energy, providing excellent corrosion resistance and wear properties, particularly for complex geometries. In contrast, electro nickel plating utilizes an electrical current to deposit nickel onto a substrate, allowing for thicker coatings and quicker processes. Both methods serve various industries, including automotive, electronics, and aerospace, enhancing component durability and performance.</p></p>
