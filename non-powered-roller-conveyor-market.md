<p><p>The non-powered roller conveyor market consists of various types, including chain-driven and belt-driven live roller conveyors. Chain-driven roller conveyors utilize chains to propel loads along the conveyor, offering robust performance for heavier items. In contrast, belt-driven live roller conveyors use belts to drive the rollers, providing a smoother and quieter operation ideal for lighter loads. Both types are essential in material handling systems, enhancing efficiency in warehouses, manufacturing, and distribution centers through effective product movement.</p></p>
