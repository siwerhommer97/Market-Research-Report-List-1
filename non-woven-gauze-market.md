<p><p>The non-woven gauze market is divided into sterile and non-sterile categories. Sterile non-woven gauze is processed to eliminate all microorganisms, making it suitable for surgical and open wound care to reduce infection risk. Non-sterile gauze, in contrast, is not treated to remove pathogens and is typically used for general wound management or dressing. The choice between these types depends on the clinical requirements, with sterile gauze preferred for critical applications and non-sterile for everyday uses.</p></p>
