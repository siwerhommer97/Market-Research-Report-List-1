<p><p>The paints and coatings market encompasses several types based on their formulation. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, providing robust durability and application versatility. Waterborne coatings are environmentally friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, thus reducing volatile organic compounds (VOCs). High-solids coatings contain a higher concentration of solids, offering improved durability with less solvent. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and cured to form a tough finish, ideal for various surfaces. Other categories include specialty coatings for unique applications.</p></p>
