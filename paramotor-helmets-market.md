<p><p>The paramotor helmets market consists of two main types: no electronics helmets and radio communication (comm) helmets. No electronics helmets are designed for basic safety, providing protection without any integrated communication features, making them lighter and often more affordable. In contrast, radio comm helmets include built-in communication systems, allowing pilots to communicate with each other and ground support while flying. This type enhances safety and coordination but typically comes with added weight and complexity due to the electronic components.</p></p>
