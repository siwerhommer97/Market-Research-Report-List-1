<p><p>Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) in the cosmetic market is categorized into fully hydrolyzed and partially hydrolyzed types, each serving distinct purposes. Fully hydrolyzed PVA offers superior film-forming properties and excellent moisture retention, making it ideal for formulations like moisturizers and skin barriers. Conversely, partially hydrolyzed PVA is more soluble in water and provides enhanced flexibility, making it suitable for hair care products and emulsions. Both types are valued for their stability, biocompatibility, and versatility in various cosmetic applications.</p></p>
