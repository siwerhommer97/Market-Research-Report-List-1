<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and Ics</li><li>Powered Device Controllers and Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) lighting market comprises two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and Integrated Circuits (ICs), and Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs. PSE controllers manage the delivery of power through Ethernet cables to connected devices, enabling efficient lighting solutions. PD controllers and ICs, on the other hand, facilitate the reception and management of power in lighting fixtures, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency. Together, these components drive the growth of smart, interconnected lighting systems.</p></p>
