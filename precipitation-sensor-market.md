<p><strong>The Precipitation Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tipping Bucket Precipitation Sensor</li><li>Weighing Precipitation Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precipitation sensor market includes two primary types: tipping bucket precipitation sensors and weighing precipitation sensors. Tipping bucket sensors measure rainfall by collecting water in a funnel that tips when a specific volume is reached, allowing for real-time data collection. Conversely, weighing precipitation sensors utilize scales to measure the accumulation of water directly, offering high accuracy for various precipitation types. Both types are essential for meteorological research, hydrology, and agricultural applications, providing critical data for weather monitoring and resource management.</p></p>
