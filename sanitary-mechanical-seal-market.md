<p><p>The Sanitary Mechanical Seal Market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mechanical Seals and Double Mechanical Seals. Single Mechanical Seals consist of one sealing face and are commonly used in applications with lower pressure and contamination risks. In contrast, Double Mechanical Seals feature two sealing faces, offering enhanced protection against leakage and contamination, making them suitable for high-pressure and critical processes. Both seal types are essential in maintaining hygiene and operational efficiency in sanitary applications across various industries.</p></p>
