<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging(FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging(FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip(FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor chip packaging market features several key technologies. Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP) allows for increased I/O connections through a larger die footprint, improving performance and miniaturization. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP) has a more traditional layout with chip connections made within the die area, optimizing space but limiting I/O. Flip Chip (FC) technology involves flipping the chip to connect directly to the substrate, enhancing thermal and electrical performance. Lastly, 2.5D/3D packaging integrates multiple chips in a compact format, boosting speed and functionality.</p></p>
