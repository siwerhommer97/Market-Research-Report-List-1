<p><p>The single cell isolation system market comprises several key types. Flow cytometry utilizes fluorescent labeling and laser technology for sorting cells based on specific characteristics. Microfluidics involves manipulating tiny fluid volumes in microchannels, allowing for precise single-cell analysis. Magnetic Activated Cell Sorting (MACS) employs magnetic beads to isolate specific cell types based on surface markers. Other methods include laser capture microdissection and manual pipetting techniques, each offering unique advantages for isolating and studying individual cells in various research and clinical applications.</p></p>
