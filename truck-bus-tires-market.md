<p><p>The Truck & Bus Tires market is primarily segmented into bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric cords laid at angles, providing a flexible sidewall and a sturdy structure, making them suitable for specific applications and cost-effective. In contrast, radial tires have cords arranged perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, improved traction, and longer tread life. Radial tires are increasingly preferred for modern trucks and buses due to their performance advantages.</p></p>
