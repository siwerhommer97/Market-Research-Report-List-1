<p><strong>The Weather Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Weather Instrument</li><li>Upper-air Weather Instrument</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The weather meter market is divided into two primary types: ground weather instruments and upper-air weather instruments. Ground weather instruments focus on surface-level measurements, collecting data on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation, essential for local weather forecasting and agriculture. In contrast, upper-air weather instruments are designed to gather information from higher altitudes, measuring atmospheric conditions such as pressure, temperature, and wind patterns. These instruments are crucial for understanding weather systems and improving aviation safety and climate research.</p></p>
